Tomato Sandwiches

When I was a kid, my family grew an abundance of tomatos every summer in the backyard. If one of us asked what we should eat for lunch, the suggestion was invariably "a tomato sandwich!"

Photo credit: Önder Örtel

Ingredients

Steps

  1. Place cheese on one slice of bread and then toast both slices of bread for 1-2 minutes.
  2. Spread mayo on the slice without cheese.
  3. Lay basil over the mayo.
  4. Lay tomato slice on top of the basil and add the salt and pepper on top of the mayo.
  5. Add the top slice of the sandwich and enjoy!
